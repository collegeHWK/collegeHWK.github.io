Slime Rancher

Slime Rancher is a cute and relaxing game about ranching in an unexplored world. You play as Beatrix LeBeau and can traverse the lands filled with bouncy slimes. With your nifty equipments in hand, you can capture slimes and keep them on your ranch. by feeding the slime with specific types of food depending on their sub-species, you can collect plorts to be sold off for money. In this sandbox-style, first-person game, you are free to do whatever you want on your ranch and beyond.

Final Verdict: It is a lovely game, within a well-created environment. However, it will eventually become too repetitive in terms of gameplay.

Bioshock 1

Bioshock 1 is a first person game based on the shooter genre. You play as Jack who was involved in a violent plane crash which resulted in him being the only survivor. You discover an entrance which leads to the hidden world of Rapture, an underwater city. You will meet a cast of characters, various enemies, and a wide range of weaponry to help your journey in this wonderous world.

Gameplay:

Final Verdict: An amazing game even though it is running on a dataed engine. The story is compelling and you need to pay close attention to details of the story.

Bioshock 2

Bioshock 2 is the sequel to Bioshock1. You return to Rapture and play as the main protagonist, an older type of "Big Daddy" that servers to protec the "Little Sisters" of Rapture. The journey this time involves finding out what has happened to your "assigned" Little Sister after Rapture has fallen to madness. Defeat countless enemies with your trust weapon and drill them to bits.

Bioshock Infinite

Bioshock Infinite is third entry to the Bioshock franchise and takes on a new theme and location. This time, you play as Booker DeWitt, who is tasked to finding Elizabeth from the floating city of Columbia. In this first person shooter game, you are to defeat enemies through the means of power and weapons in order to complete your objective.

Gameplay:

Story:


Final Verdict: This sequel comes as a suprise. Even though it has dropped themes from the original Bioshocks, it still maintained a compelling-story telling and has a much more polished gameplay mechanics. Furthermore, graphics are much better compared to previous entries.

However, in a quiet little village of Greenwood, a sacred jewel has been stolen. Anna, the village ranger sets out to recover the jewels by enlisting the help of the three memebers before the jewels are used for the wrong reasons.

This game involves 5 players who are pitted against 5 other players in an arena. Through the use of various champions who has a wide variety of skills, players must work together and strategize their gameplay in order to defeat the opponents, claim objectives, and destroy the enemy nexus.

Fallout 4 is one of the games which I'm on the fence about. This game revolves around the Lone wanderer exploring the remnants of Boston in a world ravaged by nuclear bombardment. Your goal is to find your son, who was taken from the vault just when the bombs started to fall. However, you have the freedom to complete whatever goals or quests that you want to do first. This first person shooter game also has a outpost system where you can create outposts for survivors to join.

Starcraft 2

Starcraft 2 is a real-time strategic game invovling three different races. This game comprises of 3 sections: The main campaign; The online PVP; and The custom games. The main campaign tells the story of the Terran, Zerg, and Protoss race after the events of Starcraft: Broodwar. The online PVP involves player-versus-player gameplay in which players pick a race and battle each other until one player remains. Lastly, the custom games involve player-made arcade modes and the Co=op mission modes.

Gameplay:

Final Verdict: Starcraft 2 hit the nail on many points for me. I was able to enjoy a good story in the campaign, and have fun in arcade mode with friends. Starcraft 2 is a successful sequel to the original Starcraft game.
